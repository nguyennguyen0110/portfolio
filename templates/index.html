Who I am

About me:

I'm Nguyen The Anh Nguyen, or Nguyen Nguyen for short. You can also call me Martin. I was born in 1987. I'm living in Dong Nai province, Vietnam.

My professional journey began in the printing industry, where I worked as both a graphic designer and later a production manager. Over more than a decade, I built expertise in production planning, quality control, and collaborating with international partners to implement new technologies. These experiences developed my attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and leadership abilities.

After being affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, I decided to pivot toward software development — a field that was growing rapidly at the time. I initially taught myself programming, which sparked a deep interest in software development. I later formalized my learning through a "Full Stack Java Web Developer" course at FPT Academy and a "Data Science" program at Funix. Since then, I’ve used Python with Flask to deploy machine learning models, and Java with Spring Boot to build robust backend systems.

With hands-on experience in both industrial production and backend development, I bring a unique mix of discipline, systems thinking, and technical agility.

Career direction:

I’m currently exploring opportunities in two areas where I can make a strong impact: backend development and production (engineering, planning, or management).

For roles in manufacturing or production, I’m especially drawn to work environments that emphasize clear processes, structured workflows, and precision — values I deeply admire and strive to align with. To support this, I’ve been learning Japanese via Duolingo, inspired by how these values are often reflected in Japanese work culture.

For software development roles, I’m looking for an environment that values collaboration, embraces best practices and clear communication, especially where modern tools (like ERP systems or Jira) are in place to support the workflow.

Whether in software or production, I bring discipline, adaptability, and a strong drive to contribute meaningfully.
